Hum Dono Ke Rules ❤️

1. Agar hum kabhi fight karein, to hum mein se ek pehle chup ho jayega. No unnecessary arguing or making things worse.
2. Fight ke baad, hum 1-24 hours lenge apni emotions settle karne ke liye - hum ek dusre ko space denge, distance nahi.
3. Agar kuch bhi hum mein se kisi ko uncomfortable kare, hum us baare mein baat karenge aur usse saath mein fix karenge.
4. Fight ke dauran "mujhe nahi rehna" ya "breakup kar lete hain" jaisi baatein nahi kahenge - hum dono jaante hain ki hum ek dusre ko khona nahi chahte.
5. Chahe argument kitna bhi bada ho, hum hamesha usse baat karke solve karenge together. ❤️
6. It's never you vs me, it's always us vs the problem. ❤️

These rules aren't meant to control each other - they're here to protect our relationship. ❤️
